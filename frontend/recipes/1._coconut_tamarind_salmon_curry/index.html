1. COCONUT TAMARIND SALMON CURRY

40 min

  • Salmon 100-150 g
  • Coconut milk
  • Tamarind paste
  • 2 Peppers
  • Curry leaves
  • Cardamom
  • Ginger
  • Garam masala
  • Cumin seeds
  • Lime
  • 1 onion
  • 2 pieces of ginger
  • 2 pieces of garlic
  • 1 piece of chilli
  • Coriander
  • Rice

Preparation

  1. Salmon. Clean and dry the salmon. Season salmon with salt, pepper, garlic powder, tamarind, olive oil, lime juice. Put the rice on.
  2. Spices. Cut in small pieces: 1 onion, 2 pieces of ginger, 2 pieces of garlic, 1 piece of pepper, 1 piece of chilli. Hit a pan with some butter or olive oil and put the onion, ginger and garlic. Add some cardamom and cumin seeds and heat for a few minutes. Add the pepper and the chilli. Plus, you can add some black pepper, salt, curry leaves and tamarind paste. After a few minutes, add coconut milk and spice powders (curry, garam masala) and a spoon of tomato sauce.
