3. VEGAN CASHEW TOFU WITH HOISIN SAUCE

40 min

  • 100-150 tofu
  • Avocado soy sauce
  • Cornstarch
  • Rice vinegar
  • Soy sauce (or tomari)
  • Green onion
  • 2/3 pieces of garlic
  • Roasted salted cashew 50 g
  • Sesame oil.
  • Rice
  • Broccoli
