4. ZESTY TOFU RICE BOWL

30 min

  • Tofu (150g)
  • Basmati rice
  • 1 Onion
  • Garlic
  • Oregano
  • Rosemary
  • Thyme
  • Cayenne
  • Parsley

Preparation

  1. Tofu. Cut the tofu in cubes. Put in a pin with olive oil and add some salt. Fire at mid heat for a few minutes, and then turn the tofu upside down.
  2. Prepare the spices. Wash the rice. Add 1 sliced onion and 1 sliced piece of garlic to a pin with oil, then add salt. Heat medium-high until the onion starts to caramelise (it gets blondie). Then reduce heat to medium. Add 1⁄2 spoon of oregano, rosemary and thyme and ¼ of cayenne pepper and add them to the onions. You can also add some sliced garlic. Cook for 1-2 minutes. Add 1 cup of tomato passata (250ml) and cook on medium heat until it forms a thick paste.
  3. Rice. Add the rice to the pin. Then add salt and vegetable broth (water is also fine) (500 ml/2 cups). Cook for around 20 minutes on low heat.
  4. Finishing touches. Add the tofu to the rice. You can cut some parsley and add it too. Finally, you can add pepper and mix it.
