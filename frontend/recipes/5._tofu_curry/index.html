5. TOFU CURRY

40 min

  • Tofu (200g)
  • Basmati rice
  • Grated garlic
  • Chilli (2-3 pieces)
  • White cauliflower (1)
  • Turmeric
  • Paprika
  • Coriander
  • Cinnamon
  • Nutmeg
  • Garam masala
  • Curry leaves
  • Black mustard seeds
  • Cumin seeds
  • Coconut milk
  • Spinach

Preparation

  1. Aromatics. Get the grated garlic and ginger and cut 2-3 chillies and put all together in a SCODELLA.
  2. Cut the cauliflower in small pieces.
  3. Spices. 1 sp of turmeric, paprika, Â½ sp of coriander and cinnamon and nutmeg. Also you can add curry leaves and black mustard seeds
  4. Put the basmati rice on
  5. Curry. Add oil to a pin. Then add black mustard seeds and cumin seeds (1 spoon each) and toast them for 1 minute. Now add the aromatics (garlic and ginger) and a little bit of water.. After 1-2 minutes add the spices, curry leaves and salt and pepper. Add a bin of coconut milk and tomato sauce. Also add a spoon of coconut sugar and bring to a nice simmer. After 3-4 minutes add the cauliflower and mix it softly. Leave it there for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Tofu. Rinse the tofu and cut it into boxes. Boil the tofu in water for 2-3 minutes.
  7. Chop the spinach and add it to the curry. Also you can add some garam masala and lemon. Add the tofu at the top of it.
