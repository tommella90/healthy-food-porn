6. HONEY CHICKEN WITH BROCCOLI

25 min

  • Chicken 150 g
  • 1 green broccoli
  • Rice
  • Flour
  • Soy sauce/tamari sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Honey
  • Chilli powder
  • Parsley.
  • Garlic
  • Pepper

Preparation

  1. Rice. Clean the rice and cook it in a pin or a rice-cooker.
  2. Broccoli. Cut the broccoli into small pieces (you can cut the GAMBO), clean and put them in a pin with oil. Add some pepper and chilli powder or paprika on it if you like it spicy, and add some water. Cover the pin and heat it at medium level for 15-20 minutes.
  3. Chicken. Add salt and pepper to the chicken on both sides. Then cover it with flour. Put some oil on a pin; when it's hot spread the chicken on it. Cut 2-3 garlic cloves and add it to the pin.
  4. Last touch. Now add the chicken. Then add: 1 spoon of soy sauce (or tamari), 1 spoon of vinegar and 1 spoon of honey and mix softly. Heat ad mid level for a few minutes and then turn the chicken. To finish, add some parsley and serve it with the rice and the broccoli.
