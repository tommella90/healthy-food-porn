7. QUINOA AND LENTIL SALAD

40 min

  • Quinoa 100g
  • Lentils 150-200g
  • Lemon
  • 1-2 onion
  • Carrots 200g
  • Paprika
  • Cumin
  • Cayenne pepper (optional)
  • Vegetable broth (optional)
  • Parsley and mint (optional)

Preparation

  1. Prepare lentils and quinoa (optional). Soak lentils (100g) in water for 8-10 hours (let it soak at night). Wash the quinoa (100g) in water until the water runs clear. Now soak it in water for 30 minutes.
  2. Preparation. Put the lentils in a pin and 1 spoon of salt and 2 cups of water. Bring the water to boil and then reduce heat to mid level for 5-10 minutes. Take the lentils out of the pin and let them dry. Squeeze some lemon, add some olive oil and let them marinate for a few minutes.

Heat at mid level for 5-6 minutes, until the onions and the carrots are lightly browned. - Add some grated garlic (1 spoon) and heat it for 1 minute. Reduce heat to low-medium. - - Now add tomato paste (3 spoon) - Add the spices to the pin and mix all together (1 or 2 minutes) at low heat. - Add the quinoa on top of it and 1 cup of vegetable broth (or water) - Bring it to high heat to get to a vigorous boil, then reduce the heat to low and cook for ~20 minutes.
  4. Finishing touches. Add the lentils on top of the salad. Optionals: cut some parsley and 1 onion and add them to the salad. Add some pepper and some mint. Now mix it all together and leave it there for 5 minutes. Enjoy.
