8. OATS YOGURT PUDDING

30 min

  • Oats (50g)
  • Greek yoghurt
  • 1 egg
  • Blueberries
  • Honey
  • Cinnamon (optional)

Preparation

  1. Prepare the pudding. Soak 50g of oats (30 minutes or more) and put them in a bowl for the oven. Add 50g of greek yoghurt, 1 egg, a spoon of cinnamon and some blueberries. If you want to add sweetness, you can add 1 or 2 spoons of honey. Mix everything together. You can leave everything in the bowl or prepare smaller bowls to prepare more little puddings.
  2. Heat. Prepare the oven at 180 degrees and put the bowl inside. Leave it there for 30 minutes.
  3. Finishing touch. Extract the pudding from the oven. You can add more blueberries and honey at the top, or chocolate drops. Enjoy.
